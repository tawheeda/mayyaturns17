Mhmm, my Sumayya? She’s super cool 😹❤️

I still remember the first time she saw me. My father introduced me to her and she froze. She was like, “That’s your brother, neh?” Then she looked at me, trying to process everything because we look so much alike. My dad said, “Yes,” and she went home to tell everyone she’d met her brother. Nobody understood what she was talking about 😂

Last year June, at our granny’s funeral, we met again. Mommy Thato asked me to go fetch Sumayya 😂 but my dad ended up going alone. I was asleep when she arrived, but I heard her voice. The next morning I didn’t see her and got so worried like, “Where’s my sister?” Then I saw her. She was wearing a bismillah 😂 We hugged, she held my hand and took me to the tent because everyone was there. She literally read my mind because I wanted to go where my family was.

After that we went home, ate, stocked up on snacks and energy drinks 😂 Then we were all over Mabopane streets, singing loud in the car. We got home around 5, played games at our granny’s place, and slept in my father’s room.

We cooked together and took so many pictures. ❤️
